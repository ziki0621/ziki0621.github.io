PhonoThink: Improving Large Language Model Reasoning on Chinese Phonological Ambiguities

Co-author · Advised by Prof. Emmanuele Chersoni · PolyU

Constructed a four-step reasoning chain dataset with homophone substitution. Trained Qwen2.5-7B via reinforcement learning, achieving 66.21% accuracy — approaching Qwen2.5-72B (71.86%) and significantly narrowing the model scale gap.

BlackboxNLP 2025

From BERT to LLMs: Understanding Chinese Classifier Prediction in Language Models

First Author · Advised by Prof. Emmanuele Chersoni · PolyU

Evaluated Chinese classifier generation across models using prompt engineering and LoRA fine-tuning. Found BERT's bidirectional attention (62.31%) outperforms larger models in this task, revealing insights about attention mechanisms in grammatical prediction.
