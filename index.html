About Me

◇

Nice to meet you! I'm a final-year student at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), double majoring in Linguistics and Artificial Intelligence. My research interests focus on how AI understands and applies human language. Outside of academics, I'm passionate about outdoor activities, writing, and playing music. I serve as first flute in the PolyU Orchestra and have performed at various music festivals and concerts. If we share similar interests, feel free to reach out—I would be glad to connect with you!
